Importance of Units in Calculations

Units are a fundamental aspect of calculations, though often overlooked as merely a way to earn marks. However, they play a crucial role in solving problems, especially in objective questions, in ways that might not be immediately apparent.

Consider the unit of speed, which is meters per second ms⁻¹. From this unit, we can infer that speed is the distance covered divided by the time taken because m = meters and s = seconds.Formulas can help derive units.
For example, in physics, the formula of stress is Force/ Cross sectional area
This tells us that the unit of stress is Nm⁻² since force is N (Newtons) and area is m² and it is Newtons/meters²
